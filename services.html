SERVICE_REGISTRY

Three systems.
Deploy separately or chained together.

Every service below runs on the same underlying stack — n8n orchestration, AI agents at the decision points — so they can operate standalone or feed directly into one another.

NODE_01 · LEAD GENERATION

Google Maps
Lead Scraper

WHAT IT DOES

Searches Google Maps by category, city, and radius, then pulls business name, phone number, website, rating, and review count into a clean, exportable list — no manual copy-pasting.

WHO IT'S FOR

Agencies and sales teams who need a steady pipeline of qualified local leads — e-commerce service providers, B2B outreach teams, and local business consultants.

EXECUTION LOG

> query: "restaurants in Lahore"  →  340 results found
> filtered by rating < 4.2  →  87 qualified leads
> export: leads_lahore_restaurants.csv  [done]

NODE_02 · CUSTOMER RESPONSE

WhatsApp &
Voice Agent

WHAT IT DOES

An AI voice and chat agent — built on Vapi and orchestrated through n8n — answers inbound calls and WhatsApp messages, qualifies the customer, books meetings straight into your calendar, and logs every conversation to a shared sheet.

WHO IT'S FOR

Local service businesses that lose leads outside business hours — clinics, salons, contractors, restaurants — anywhere a missed call is a missed sale.

EXECUTION LOG

> inbound_call received, 9:42 PM  →  agent answered in 1 ring
> intent classified: booking_request
> calendar.event.create()  [booked]  →  lead saved to CRM

NODE_03 · AUDIENCE GROWTH

Content &
Social Automation

WHAT IT DOES

A recurring pipeline that scripts, generates, and schedules content across social channels — keeping your accounts active without a content team producing every post by hand.

WHO IT'S FOR

Content creators and e-commerce brands who need consistent posting cadence but don't have the bandwidth to script and edit daily.

EXECUTION LOG

> brief: "weekly product highlight"  →  script generated
> render: cinematic clip, 16:9  [done]
> scheduled: Mon 9:00 AM, 3 platforms
